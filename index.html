Press Biography Brief

Preston Lee is former CEO and founding member of OpenRain Software, acquired by redPear of Tempe, Arizona in Q4, 2009. A veteran entrepreneur, he has provided senior leadership for a gamut of client companies making visions of engaging, custom, dynamic software systems a reality. He has become recognized for a lighthearted balance of technical savvy, business wisdom and customer outlooks and as a technological innovator, pragmatic entrepreneure and business leader. Preston actively seeks opportunities to guide organizations with Internet- oriented solutions. He holds MBA and BS degrees, and supports his alma maters with mentoring, community participation and financial contributions.

Preston speaks regularly at local and national events on business, entrepreneurship, software development, technology trends, computing best practices, web design, Internet marketing, and social media.

While not on the job, Preston is active in many local technology communities and enjoys woodworking, music performance, and filling frames on his SLR.
